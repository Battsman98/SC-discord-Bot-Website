Getting started

  1. Choose a tool from the Overview page or the navigation bar.
  2. Enter the information you know. Optional filters narrow large result lists.
  3. Select an available result or action to view details, save an item, or update your records.

Your Discord sign-in connects saved hangar, blueprint, inventory, timer, and permission data to your server identity. Some browsing tools can be used without elevated access; editing and administrative controls depend on your Discord roles.

Ships & Hangar

Ship Search finds ships and vehicles by name, role, or keyword. Use manufacturer, vehicle type, size, and status filters to narrow results, then sort by name, manufacturer, size, or cargo capacity.

Hangar stores your fleet. Add ships from search results, refresh the list after a change, and use quantity controls when you own more than one.

Import RSI Hangar imports eligible vehicles from standalone pledges and game packages. With the optional importer extension installed, stay signed in to RSI and select the import button. Otherwise, upload saved RSI pledge-page HTML files when prompted. Paints, upgrades, equipment, flair, and other extras are ignored.

Trade

Commodity Lookup shows places to buy and sell a named commodity. Add purchase or sell systems when you want results limited to a region.

Trade Routing builds candidate routes from a starting terminal or location. Enter your ship, investment, and maximum stops. “Stay in system” keeps the route local. Prices and availability can change, so verify them in game before committing credits.

Mining

Mining Locator searches for a material and lists matching planets, moons, Lagrange points, or points of interest. Add a system or location filter when you know where you want to operate.

Update Material Location lets authorized community members report a useful location. Choose the correct location type and enter its in-game name carefully. Community additions appear in future mining results.

Industry Operations keeps three common crew tasks together. Use Crew Payout after selling cargo to subtract shared expenses and divide the net amount evenly. Names earlier in the list receive any indivisible one-aUEC remainder.

Use Refinery Completion with the remaining duration shown in game. SC Companion converts it to your local completion time and provides a Discord timestamp that displays correctly for every crew member's time zone.

Use Operation Brief before launch to assemble the objective, rally point, crew or open roles, and important notes. Copy the finished brief into Discord. The same workflows are available through /industry split, /industry refinery, and /industry brief.

Blueprints

Blueprint Search finds crafting blueprints by item name, category, required material, mission type, contractor, or location. Results can include crafting requirements and mission sources.

My Blueprints keeps your saved collection together. Add or remove blueprints from their result controls and refresh the library after updates.

Blueprint Import can capture a shared game window, read uploaded screenshots, or match pasted text. Review matches before saving—image text recognition may need correction when the source is small or blurred.

Items

Item Locator searches equipment and components. Search by item name or keyword, then narrow by section, category, or size. Open a result for available item details and location information.

Inventory

Station Inventory lists saved items. Search names and notes, filter by location or category, and sort the table. Export Excel downloads a portable copy. “Clear Station” removes the selected location; “Clear All” removes every saved entry. Both ask for confirmation.

Add Inventory Item records an item manually with its category, type, size, location, quantity, quality, SCU volume, and notes. Item name, location, and quantity are required.

Inventory Import reads item names from a shared Star Citizen window. Enter the destination, choose a category, share the game window, start scanning, and hover over each item for a few seconds. Stop when finished and review matches before importing. Advanced settings adjust scan timing, match strictness, guide position, ignored words, and manual OCR troubleshooting.

Timers

Executive Hangar displays the current community clock. Authorized members can set the phase and remaining minutes when the state needs correction, or clear a manual override.

Contested Zone Timers displays active zone countdowns and their current state. Changes made through connected Discord tools appear here after refresh.

Access, permissions & safety

Sign in with Discord for personal saved data or role-protected actions. Server roles determine whether you can add mining locations, change Executive Hangar timing, or view Audit events. Audit is visible only to authorized administrators.

This is an unofficial community tool. Game data, prices, availability, and schedules may change, so confirm important decisions in game. Never share your Discord password, RSI password, session cookie, or account credentials with the website.
