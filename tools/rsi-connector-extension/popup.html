RSI Hangar Importer

Open Game Assist and click Import RSI Hangar. The extension processes your RSI hangar locally and sends only recognized ship and vehicle names.

It does not send your RSI password, session cookie, full pledge pages, billing information, CCUs, paints, equipment, or other extras.
