# Game Assist RSI Hangar Importer Privacy Policy

Effective July 20, 2026. The extension locally processes RSI My Hangar responses after a user starts an import and sends only recognized ship and vehicle names to SC Companion.

It does not transmit complete RSI HTML, passwords, session-cookie values, authorization headers, billing or payment information, addresses, orders, pledge identifiers, buybacks, CCUs, paints, equipment, weapons, armor, flair, currency, coupons, gift cards, or unrelated browsing history.

Imported records remain in SC Companion until removed by the user. They are not sold or used for advertising, credit decisions, or unrelated purposes.

Public policy: https://sccompanion.org/rsi-hangar-importer/privacy

Support: https://github.com/Battsman98/SC-discord-Bot-Website/issues
