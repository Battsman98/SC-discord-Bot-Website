Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Game Assist Bot handles information while providing Discord-based in-game assistance.

Information the Bot May Process

The bot may process Discord command input, Discord user IDs, server IDs, channel IDs, message IDs, and command metadata when needed to respond to commands, troubleshoot errors, prevent abuse, or operate bot features.

Information Stored

The bot may store temporary cached lookup results from configured websites or APIs. The bot is not intended to store private message content, passwords, payment information, or sensitive personal information.

Logs

The bot may generate operational logs, such as startup events, command errors, and connection status. Logs are used to maintain and debug the bot.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to respond to Discord commands, provide requested game information, improve reliability, prevent misuse, and maintain bot security.

The companion website uses a first-party browser identifier to count daily page views and approximate unique browsers. The server stores only a one-way hash of that random identifier, does not store IP addresses for analytics, and reports aggregate visitor counts only to authorized administrators.

Sharing

The bot does not sell personal information. Information may be sent to Discord as part of normal bot operation and may be requested from configured third-party websites or APIs when performing lookups.

Data Retention

Cached lookup results and logs may be retained for a limited period as needed for performance, troubleshooting, and security. Server administrators may remove the bot from a server at any time.

Children

The bot is intended for use within Discord and is not directed at children under the minimum age required by Discord's Terms of Service.

Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect bot credentials and operational data. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the bot after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the bot owner through the Discord server where the bot is used.
